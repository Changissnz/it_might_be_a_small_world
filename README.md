# It Might Be a Small World 

It's nothing personal. It's not business either. And I'm not Italian.  
Written by Richard Pham. 
---------------------------------------------------------------------

It might be a small world.
But it still isn't owned by the Judeans,
the Brittanians, the Germanians,
the Schinians, the Schlavians,
or the Americans.
The world operates by itself, as one.

When people choose to separate,
that gives them the vantage point.
From that vantage point,
they go on to paint convincing worldviews
defined by their brands and status.
It's mostly relative.
There are rare parts that are not.

Do not condone torture,
and then act stupified if you're stupid.
If you're smart, do the opposite.
If that's the rules you want to play by,
do you understand now?
We can never be friends.
Your business will die.
Your people will be forced on the retreat.
Or you and your people will be slaughtered.

You're a son of a fucking bitch, brother.
I hope you die.
I hear you talking shit about me in my sleep.
Don't make me hunt you down
and burn you alive.
